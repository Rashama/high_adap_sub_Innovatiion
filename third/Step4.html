Step 4: Solutions to Help Less Productive Employees

Less productive employees have high rates of project failure, absenteeism, and customer complaints. Could you suggest some solutions to help them? Please provide at least three solutions.


These are some examples. Please come up with your own ideas.

  • 1) Help them to understand their value and reward exceptional efforts.
  • 2) Don't let achievements go unnoticed.
  • 4) Call out impressive work in a monthly newsletter, on your webpage, or at a company meeting.
  • 5) Make the employees aware of your expectations.
  • 6) Incentivize the results you want to see.
  • 7) Share continuity plans with staff and implement their feedback.
  • 8) Give them a reputation to live up to.
  • 9) Ensure staff working early or late have a plan in place in case something happens.
