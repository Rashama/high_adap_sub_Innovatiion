Informed Consent Form
Please read this consent form before you decide to participate in this study.

Purpose of the research study
We are conducting an online experiment to understand how people use and adapt to technology. This research is being conducted at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. We would appreciate your participation in this study.

Risks/Discomforts
Risks are minimum. No sensitive data will be collected. Your ID will only be used for distributing your compensation.

Benefits
You will not receive any direct benefits from your participation. However, we hope that others will benefit from the findings of this work.

Voluntary participation
Your participation is completely voluntary. You can leave the task at any time.

Confidentiality
Your identity will be kept strictly confidential. The collected data will be securely stored and viewed only by the researchers. The University of Michigan Health Sciences and Behavioral Sciences Institutional Review Board has determined that this study is exempt from IRB oversight.

Questions about the research
If you have questions regarding this study you may contact Rasha Alahmad at rashama@umich.edu
